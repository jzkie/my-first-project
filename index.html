Barista


  • BARISTA
    • -A barista is a person, usually a coffeehouse employee,
    who prepares and serves espresso-based coffee drinks.
    In coffee shops, over the counter employees are referred to as "baristas",
    although the preparation process is fully automated

    Type of drinks

    1.Espresso



  • Espresso is a coffee-brewing method of Italian origin, in which a small amount of nearly boiling water (about 90 °C or 190 °F) is forced


  • Espresso is by far the most popular coffee drink around the world, not just in the US.
    Many people enjoy a single or double shot of espresso on its own, especially as an after-dinner drink.
    But espresso also forms the base of many of your favorite coffee drinks, too, so it’s incredibly versatile, not to mention delicious!
